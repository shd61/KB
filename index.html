Ayurveda Pallekele

Construction Project: Ayurveda Pallekele Roofing Repair
Project Overview: The Ayurveda Pallekele Roofing Repair project is a comprehensive roofing restoration and repair initiative in the serene location of Pallekele, Sri Lanka. The objective of this project is to refurbish and strengthen the roofing structures of the Ayurveda facility, ensuring the preservation of its cultural heritage and enhancing its safety and functionality.

Project Duration:
Start Date: January 2023
End Date: January 2024

Scope of Work:
Roof Inspection and Assessment:
A thorough assessment of the existing roofing condition will be conducted to identify areas in need of repair or replacement.
Roof Repair and Restoration: Damaged roofing materials will be replaced, and necessary repairs will be carried out to restore the structural integrity of the roofing system.
Waterproofing and Insulation: High-quality waterproofing and insulation measures will be applied to ensure the facility's protection against moisture and temperature fluctuations.
Enhancing Aesthetics: The roofing will be aesthetically enhanced to maintain its traditional charm while embracing modern design elements.
Safety Upgrades: Safety features such as guardrails and handrails will be added to ensure a safe environment for visitors and staff.

Peradeniya University Wall Construction Project (August - December 2022)

We are proud to announce the successful completion of the Peradeniya University Wall Construction Project, which took place from August to December 2022. This significant endeavor aimed to enhance the security and aesthetics of the university campus while fostering a conducive environment for learning and research.

Project Highlights:
Start Date: August 2022
End Date: December 2022

Project Overview:
During this four-month period, our dedicated team worked tirelessly to construct a robust and visually appealing boundary wall for the Peradeniya University campus. The new wall not only ensures the safety and privacy of the university community but also adds a touch of elegance to the campus surroundings.
Key Achievements:
Completion of the wall within the stipulated timeframe, ensuring minimal disruption to university activities.
Incorporation of modern architectural designs and construction techniques to create a durable and attractive boundary.
Collaboration with skilled artisans and construction experts to ensure high-quality workmanship.

Peradeniya Botanical Garden Road Construction Project (March - July 2022)

We are pleased to announce the successful completion of the Peradeniya Botanical Garden Road Construction Project, which took place from March to July 2022. This project aimed to enhance accessibility and visitor experience within one of Sri Lanka's most cherished natural treasures.

Project Highlights:
Start Date: March 2022
End Date: July 2022
Project Overview:
Over the course of five months, our dedicated team embarked on the mission to upgrade and revamp the road infrastructure within the Peradeniya Botanical Garden. Our goal was to provide visitors with safe and convenient pathways while preserving the Garden's natural beauty.

Key Achievements:

Completion of road construction and improvements on schedule, with minimal disturbance to the Garden's flora and fauna.
Integration of eco-friendly construction practices to ensure minimal environmental impact.
Collaboration with landscape architects to harmonize road design with the Garden's aesthetics.
